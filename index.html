How it Works

1

Feed

Intake of seawater / brackish water. Coarse protection against debris, flow stabilisation. No heavy pre-treatment.

2

Gas-sorbent

Inert carrier gas with proprietary sorbent is dosed into the flow. Active capture centres are formed.

3

Contact

Intensive mass transfer: salt ions and dissolved organics bind to sorbent. No high pressures, no boiling.

4

Separation

Fast phase separation via mechanical means. No reverse osmosis. Treated stream goes to final polishing.

5

Mineralisation

Precise remineralisation to drinking profile (TDS ~100–150 mg/L). pH 7.2–7.8. Ready-to-drink water.

6

Regeneration Loop

Closed loop regeneration. Reagents recycled. No toxic brine discharge. Low energy input.
