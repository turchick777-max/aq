Technology

Gas-sorption desalination platform

AQUINTAQA uses a proprietary gas-sorption process to selectively bind and remove salts and critical impurities, producing stable premium-grade drinking water.

Instead of relying solely on membranes or thermal processes, AQUINTAQA introduces a controlled gas phase into the reactor. The gas interacts with dissolved salts and specific contaminants, forming compounds that can be efficiently removed from the water stream.

The platform is modular and can be integrated into existing water infrastructure or deployed as standalone units in remote or off-grid locations.

01

Feed water intake

Seawater or highly mineralized feedstock is pre-filtered and fed into the AQUINTAQA reactor.

02

Gas-sorption reaction

A precise gas mixture is injected, selectively binding salts and target impurities within the reactor volume.

03

Separation & conditioning

Bound contaminants are removed. The treated water is stabilized and can be remineralized if required.

04

Premium drinking water

Output water quality is tuned to meet and exceed WHO / EU / US EPA benchmarks for drinking water.
